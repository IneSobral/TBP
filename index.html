About us ...

The Breakfast Power is a restaurant with three locations in Lisbon, all specialized in offering the best brunch in the city. With a cozy atmosphere and a creative menu, it provides a wide selection of dishes, including vegetarian and vegan options. The friendly and relaxed service is the brand's trademark, making each visit a memorable experience. Each location has its own personality, but they all share the same inviting and high-quality spirit.

Menu

Brunch is a meal that combines elements of breakfast and lunch, offering a variety of sweet and savory dishes, beverages, and a relaxed atmosphere, ideal for those seeking a complete and relaxing gastronomic experience.
